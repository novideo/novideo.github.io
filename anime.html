This is some of my favorite anime of all time. I haven't been watching anime for that long, with my first one being a Studio Ghibli film (Spirited Away). The genres I enjoy watching are usually drama, action, and comedy.

Note: the reviews will cover the entire series, not just the specific TV series/movie/ONA

You can find my entire anime list which is on AniList.

#1: Evangelion

This is hands down my favorite anime of all time. Sure there are plenty of mecha anime out there including Darling in the FranXX and Code Geass which are very good, but Evangelion has a unique psychological factor that makes it stand out. There are a lot of talks relating to the ambiguity of the show, but I think this is the beauty of it. It allows for the watcher to think and interpret for themselves what the show really means. This accompanied by the OST composed mostly by Shiro SAGISU really makes it an amazing show. Also, I appreciate the use of classical works from Bach and Beethoven. The baroque and classical works they included really suited the show. It's a really minor detail, but it really does make people especially classical music enjoyers appreciate the show.

#2: Gintama

"Gintama??? Why is this second? It's just one of those shows with 90% filler and no plot, right?"

... is what I thought too. However, there's more to it than that. Behind the comedy of Gintama is a great story that describes each character in great detail, especially the main characters. Most of the arcs introduce the watcher to a new character and their lore. This allows the watcher to develop a great understanding and connection to each of the characters. Finally, all of this comes back to the watcher when they start the Shogun Assassination arc.

Gintama usually isn't an anime I'd recommend to friends, joking aside. These days, I can't even finish Ya Boi Kongming without taking four months. However, if you have the patience, want to watch something comedic, sad, and with amazing lore, Gintama might be for you.
